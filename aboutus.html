About Us

Camgar’s Collection

Camgar’s Collection is not just a store. it's a story of resilience and passion. Started by my good friend during the tough times of the pandemic in 2020, Camgar’s Collection has become a hub for sneaker lovers in our community. The store boasts a diverse range of sneakers, featuring big names such as Nike and Adidas. Our primary focus is on catering to the local shoe enthusiasts right here in Barangay Gusa. Conveniently situated, we're here to meet the footwear needs of our neighbors, offering both style and quality under one roof.
